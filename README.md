Our project develops a Deep Learning model (specifically, a 1D Convolutional Neural Network - CNN) to automate the discovery of exoplanets from NASA's large astronomical datasets (Kepler/TESS).

The model analyses stellar light curves (time-series plots of star brightness) by focusing on the tiny, periodic dimming caused by an orbiting planet (transit signature). We use specialized data processing, including light curve folding, to isolate and enhance the subtle transit signal.

Our solution bypasses the slow, manual inspection process, providing an accurate and rapid classification of candidates into Confirmed Transit or False Positive. The final product is a deployable AI tool capable of accelerating scientific discovery in planetary science.
